Lines of Action

A board game based off checkers created in the 1960s.

Get started Game info

  1. Objective

    To Finish the game

    Get all your pieces to be connected by any direction.

  2. Rules

    Through the game

    • A piece can move in any directio
    • It must move by the number of piece a direction's line
    • You can not jump over opponent pieces
    • You can jump over own
    • You can capture your opponent piece
